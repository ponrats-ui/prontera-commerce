## Governance Model

Prontera Commerce follows a Human-in-the-Loop governance model.

AI agents may assist with:

- Analysis.
- Recommendations.
- Documentation.
- Customer support.
- Merchant onboarding.
- Low-risk automation.

But human approval is required for:

- Financial decisions.
- Legal decisions.
- Policy changes.
- Pricing changes.
- Merchant suspension.
- Marketplace moderation.
- Strategic roadmap changes.
- Irreversible data operations.

Founder final authority:

The Founder retains final decision-making authority across the platform.

## Future AI Agent Governance

Future architecture may include dedicated entities for AI agent governance:

- `Agent`
- `AgentRole`
- `AgentPermission`
- `AgentActionLog`
- `AgentApprovalRequest`

These entities are future roadmap items only. They should not be implemented as database models until the approval workflows, audit requirements, and product requirements are finalized.
