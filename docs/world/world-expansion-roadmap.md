# Prontera World Expansion Roadmap

## Purpose

Prontera begins in Merchant City but does not end there.

The World Expansion track turns a single commerce town into a connected
regional network where culture, architecture, economic identity, leadership,
and merchant discovery change with place.

This roadmap is separate from the Merchant OS and Revenue Era tracks. Merchant
operations remain professional and auditable while the buyer layer becomes
warm, explorable, and emotionally memorable.

## Regional Network

The initial network contains:

1. Merchant City — Commerce Capital
2. Harbor Kingdom — Logistics and global trade
3. Tech Republic — Technology and innovation
4. Artisan Valley — Craft, coffee, food, and handmade goods
5. Creator Island — Media, education, design, and creator businesses
6. Industrial District — Factories, machinery, wholesale, and B2B commerce

Future routes are represented as locked destinations rather than pretending the
world is already complete.

## Product Rule

Every world feature must strengthen real merchant discovery.

The world must not become an unrelated game layer. Exploration should reveal
people, products, stories, live commerce, and business identity.
