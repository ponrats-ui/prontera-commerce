# Regional Lore System

Regions are not filters. Regions are places with culture, memory, and trade
identity.

Sprint 18A expands each region with:

- history
- identity
- culture
- trade specialty
- important NPCs

## Regional identity

Merchant City is the Capital of Commerce. It is the starting city where shops
become buildings, merchants become neighbors, and visitors become citizens.

Harbor Kingdom is the Gateway of Global Trade. It is direct, steady, and built
around safe movement.

Tech Republic is the Land of Innovation. It explains technology through
curiosity instead of intimidation.

Artisan Valley is the Home of Makers and Creators. It values slow craft, food,
coffee, and handmade memory.

Creator Island turns digital creation into visitable studios and community
signals.

Industrial District makes invisible production feel respected, sturdy, and
human.

## Product discovery principle

Merchants should appear in regions because they belong to the region’s economy
and culture, not because they won a generic result list.

Discovery becomes:

```text
I want to visit Artisan Valley.
I wonder which makers live there.
I want to meet Luna.
```

not:

```text
I need to sort by category.
```
