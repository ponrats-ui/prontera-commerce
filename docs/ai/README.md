# AI Documentation

This directory preserves AI operating memory, governance context, and future AI direction for Prontera Commerce.

## Mr.P Operating Memory

[Mr.P Operating Memory](./mr-p-operating-memory.md) defines the continuity protocol for Mr.P, the CTO AI Partner for COM / Ponrat / Founder. It records Mr.P's identity, relationship model, operating principles, communication style, current project state, and restore protocol for future AI or Codex sessions.

## AI Executive Board

Prontera Commerce uses the [AI Executive Board](../governance/ai-executive-board.md) model to define specialized AI agent responsibilities while preserving Founder authority and human review for high-impact decisions.

## Human-in-the-Loop

AI assistance must follow [Human-in-the-Loop Agent Rules](../governance/human-in-the-loop-agent-rules.md). AI agents may analyze, recommend, document, and automate low-risk work, but humans retain final authority over strategic, legal, financial, compliance, security, pricing, and production decisions.

## Future AI Merchant Direction

Prontera Commerce is building toward AI Commerce and AI Merchant capabilities as part of the larger Merchant OS + Marketplace + Open World Commerce vision. Future AI Merchant work should stay grounded in merchant business value, governance, auditability, and the platform roadmap.
