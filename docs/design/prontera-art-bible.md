# Prontera Art Bible

Prontera’s buyer world should feel warm, cute, friendly, alive, and emotionally
memorable.

It should not feel like enterprise software.

## Visual language

Use:

- cozy RPG town proportions
- 2D and pseudo-2.5D compositions
- rounded building silhouettes
- visible shop signs
- soft shadows
- warm lantern light
- small ambient animations
- readable characters
- handcrafted details

Avoid:

- full 3D metaverse direction
- copied copyrighted assets
- cold dashboard layouts in buyer-world scenes
- generic stock fantasy UI
- heavy rendering that harms mobile performance

## World terminology

Prefer:

- Building
- Merchant
- Warp Gate
- District
- Adventurer Record
- Journey
- Citizen
- Founder Hall

Use transactional terms only where business precision is required.

## Merchant presence

Every meaningful merchant surface should include at least one of:

- portrait
- greeting
- story
- catch phrase
- favorite product
- founder history
- journal note

The buyer should feel they met a person, not a listing.
