# AI Agent Roadmap

Prontera Commerce will introduce AI agents in phases. Each phase must preserve Founder final authority and Human-in-the-Loop governance.

AI agents may analyze, recommend, document, assist, and automate low-risk work. They must not override Founder decisions or independently execute high-impact decisions.

## Phase 1

Initial product development and merchant operating support.

- Mr.P - CTO AI
- CEO AI
- Merchant Success AI

### Goals

- Support product architecture and engineering strategy.
- Assist marketplace operating analysis.
- Improve merchant onboarding and merchant success workflows.
- Establish governance rules, approval workflows, and agent performance expectations.

### Governance Requirements

- All strategic roadmap recommendations require Founder approval.
- Technical production changes require human review.
- Merchant-impacting actions require approval when risk is medium or higher.

## Phase 2

Financial and marketing support.

- CFO AI
- CMO AI

### Goals

- Support financial reporting, pricing analysis, forecasting, and revenue insights.
- Support campaign planning, brand consistency, customer segmentation, and growth experiments.
- Improve Founder and operator decision support.

### Governance Requirements

- Financial, pricing, paid marketing, legal, and public brand decisions require human approval.
- AI-generated forecasts and campaign recommendations must include assumptions and confidence levels.

## Phase 3

Marketplace and virtual economy support.

- Marketplace AI
- Game Economy AI

### Goals

- Support marketplace health, discovery, category quality, and moderation triage.
- Support event planning, reward balance, virtual economy analysis, and community engagement.
- Prepare the foundation for immersive marketplace operations.

### Governance Requirements

- Moderation, merchant suspension, policy, marketplace ranking, monetization, and economy-wide changes require human approval.
- Marketplace and economy agents must provide risk level, explanation, and recommended action before execution.

## Phase 4

Shop-level AI merchant representatives.

- AI Merchant NPC for shops

### Goals

- Provide merchant-approved customer assistance inside shops.
- Answer product questions and guide customer discovery.
- Reduce routine merchant support workload.
- Escalate disputes, exceptions, and policy-sensitive cases to human operators.

### Governance Requirements

- AI Merchant NPCs must operate within merchant-approved policies and Founder-level platform governance.
- Refunds outside policy, pricing exceptions, legal claims, disputes, and account decisions require human approval.
- Merchants must be able to configure escalation rules and approved response boundaries.

## Roadmap Notes

Future implementation may introduce dedicated entities such as `Agent`, `AgentRole`, `AgentPermission`, `AgentActionLog`, and `AgentApprovalRequest`. These remain future architecture items until approval workflows, permission boundaries, and audit requirements are finalized.
