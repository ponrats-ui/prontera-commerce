# Merchant Personas

## Purpose

These personas guide early merchant recruitment for the Founder Merchant 100 Program.

They help Prontera prioritize merchants who can benefit from merchant identity, world discovery, live commerce, CRM, promotions, and subscription-based growth.

## Computer Shop Owner

Profile:

A local computer shop selling PCs, notebooks, accessories, repairs, upgrades, and IT services.

Needs:

- Product catalog for hardware and services
- Trust-building storefront
- Promotion campaigns
- Customer CRM
- Live demos or product explanation
- Better local discovery

Prontera value:

The shop can become a trusted technology destination inside Tech Bazaar, with Founder recognition and future Official Store potential.

Best acquisition channels:

- Facebook Groups
- Direct Outreach
- Industry Associations
- YouTube

## Coffee Shop Owner

Profile:

A neighborhood cafe or specialty coffee shop with a strong local identity and customer relationships.

Needs:

- Brand storytelling
- Menu and product showcase
- Community discovery
- Promotions
- Events and live content
- Customer retention

Prontera value:

The shop can establish a warm merchant presence in a commerce district and build a deeper relationship with customers beyond delivery apps or social feeds.

Best acquisition channels:

- Local Business Communities
- TikTok
- Facebook Groups
- Direct Outreach

## Handmade Creator

Profile:

An artisan, craft seller, designer, or maker selling handmade products through social channels, markets, or small online shops.

Needs:

- Visual shop identity
- Product storytelling
- Customer trust
- Promotion tools
- Simple onboarding
- Discovery beyond algorithm feeds

Prontera value:

The creator can become part of Artisan Valley, where product identity and maker stories matter as much as price.

Best acquisition channels:

- TikTok
- Facebook Groups
- YouTube
- Founder Referral Program

## SME Retail Merchant

Profile:

A small or medium retailer selling physical products across offline and online channels.

Needs:

- Product management
- Inventory visibility
- Orders and POS
- Customer CRM
- Promotions
- Staff and growth readiness

Prontera value:

Prontera provides a Merchant OS foundation with a path toward world discovery, media placement, and paid subscription value.

Best acquisition channels:

- Local Business Communities
- Industry Associations
- Direct Outreach
- Facebook Groups

## Online Seller

Profile:

A seller already using marketplaces, social commerce, or chat-based selling.

Needs:

- Independent store identity
- Better customer ownership
- Promotion and voucher tools
- CRM
- Alternative discovery channels
- Lower dependence on marketplace algorithms

Prontera value:

The seller can move from listing products on other platforms to building a merchant-owned presence inside Prontera.

Best acquisition channels:

- TikTok
- Facebook Groups
- YouTube
- Founder Referral Program

## Persona Prioritization

Early recruitment should favor merchants who:

- Can add real products quickly
- Have existing customers or community
- Care about brand identity
- Are willing to give feedback
- Can become visible case studies
- Understand that Prontera is building for the long term
