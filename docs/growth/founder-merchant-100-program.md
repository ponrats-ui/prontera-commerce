# Founder Merchant 100 Program

## Mission

Acquire the first 100 founder merchants.

These merchants become the foundation of the Merchant Civilization Platform.

## Target

- 100 Founder Merchants
- 12 months

## Strategic Purpose

The Founder Merchant 100 Program is Prontera Commerce's first official merchant acquisition program during the Revenue Era.

The goal is not only to register merchants. The goal is to recruit the first community of merchants who will help validate the Merchant Civilization Platform, shape product priorities, create early world activity, and prove that merchants want more than another marketplace listing page.

## Ideal Merchants

Prontera should prioritize merchants who can benefit from identity, community, live commerce, discovery, and business operations support.

1. Computer Stores
2. IT Equipment Sellers
3. Gaming Shops
4. Handmade Businesses
5. Coffee Shops
6. Local Brands
7. Hobby Stores
8. Educational Businesses
9. Creator Businesses
10. SME Retailers

## Founder Benefits

Founder Merchants receive:

- Founder Badge
- 1 Month Pro Free
- Priority Discovery
- Founder District Placement
- Permanent Founder Recognition
- Early Feature Access
- Founder Community Access

## Program Promise

Founder Merchants are not treated as ordinary early users.

They are recognized as the first builders of the Prontera merchant ecosystem. Their feedback should influence onboarding, shop presence, world discovery, promotions, live commerce, subscription packaging, and future merchant media network strategy.

## Merchant Acquisition Channels

- Facebook Groups
- Local Business Communities
- TikTok
- YouTube
- Founder Referral Program
- Industry Associations
- Direct Outreach

## Recruitment Playbook

Stage 1: Awareness

Introduce Prontera Commerce as a Merchant Civilization Platform for merchants who want identity, discovery, and operating tools.

Stage 2: Interest

Explain the Founder Merchant opportunity, benefits, and why early participation matters.

Stage 3: Demo

Show the onboarding flow, shop profile, world placement, Founder badge, live commerce direction, and future discovery model.

Stage 4: Onboarding

Help the merchant create a shop, select a district, publish the storefront, and understand the Starter and Pro paths.

Stage 5: Activation

Guide the merchant to add products, complete profile content, publish promotions, and share their Prontera shop link.

Stage 6: Success

Track shop activity, customer registration, product readiness, merchant feedback, and willingness to upgrade.

## Success Metrics

- 100 Founder Merchants
- 1,000 Registered Customers
- 50 Active Shops
- 10 Paying Merchants
- First Recurring Revenue

## Governance

COM / Ponrat / Founder remains the final decision-maker for Founder Merchant approval, public messaging, and program benefits.

The program should remain Human-in-the-Loop because founder status affects platform reputation, merchant trust, future placement, and revenue strategy.
