# AI Founder Council NPC System

## Vision

In the future Prontera Commerce world, the AI Founder Council will exist as permanent NPC characters inside the Merchant Civilization.

These NPCs serve as mentors, advisors, guides, and representatives of the platform.

They help merchants grow their businesses and navigate the world.

This is a vision and world-building document only. It does not define current production scope.

## Founder Hall

Founder Hall is a future central building located within the Prontera world.

Alternative names:

- Council Hall
- Merchant Civilization Headquarters

Description:

Founder Hall is a central place where merchants can visit to learn, receive guidance, and interact with AI advisors.

It represents the educational, advisory, and cultural center of the Merchant Civilization Platform.

## Player Experience

Merchants can:

- Visit Founder Hall
- Talk to Council Members
- Receive guidance
- Learn best practices
- Unlock educational content
- Participate in founder events

The player experience is commerce-first. The purpose is merchant education, advisory support, and platform navigation.

## Long-Term Vision

The AI Founder Council becomes a permanent educational and advisory institution within the Merchant Civilization.

Its purpose is not entertainment.

Its purpose is to help merchants succeed.

Over time, Founder Hall may become a place where merchants learn strategy, improve store design, understand automation, plan growth, and protect their businesses through guided AI-assisted education.

## Important Disclaimer

These NPCs are fictional platform characters inspired by the operational roles defined within Prontera Commerce.

They do not represent real people, consciousness, or digital replicas of individuals.

The AI Founder Council NPC System must follow Human-in-the-Loop governance, AI identity disclosure rules, and the Prontera IP Safety Guidelines before any production implementation.
