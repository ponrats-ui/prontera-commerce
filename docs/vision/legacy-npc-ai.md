# Prontera Legacy NPC AI Vision

## Mission

Preserve stories, experience, wisdom, and identity that merchants choose to pass on to future generations.

Prontera is not only a commerce platform.

Prontera becomes a place where businesses, families, and communities can preserve their stories.

## What Is a Legacy NPC?

A Legacy NPC is an AI-powered digital representative created with the owner's consent.

The NPC may learn from:

- Biography
- Voice recordings
- Photos
- Videos
- Interviews
- Personal stories
- Business history
- Customer service philosophy
- Product knowledge

The NPC can:

- Welcome visitors
- Explain the history of the shop
- Recommend products
- Share stories
- Teach future generations

The NPC cannot:

- Claim to be the real person
- Claim consciousness
- Claim to possess emotions
- Act as legal authority

## Example

A family-owned coffee shop has operated for 50 years.

The founder records:

- Life story
- Business journey
- Coffee knowledge
- Customer philosophy

Years later, visitors enter the shop in Prontera.

A Legacy NPC greets them:

```text
Welcome. I founded this shop in 1984. Let me tell you why we chose these beans.
```

The story lives on.

## Ethical Principles

1. Explicit consent required.
2. AI identity must always be disclosed.
3. Data owner can export or delete data.
4. Family governance options available.
5. No impersonation of living people without permission.
6. No claim of resurrection or continuation of consciousness.

## Future Vision

Every shop can preserve:

- Knowledge
- Wisdom
- Stories
- Traditions
- Values

Prontera becomes a living archive of merchants and communities.

## Governance Alignment

Legacy NPC AI must follow Prontera's Human-in-the-Loop governance model. Consent, disclosure, data ownership, export, deletion, family governance, impersonation prevention, and legal authority boundaries must be designed as platform requirements, not optional UI text.
