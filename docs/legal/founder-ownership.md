# Founder Ownership

Prontera Commerce is Founder-led. The Founder retains ownership and final decision-making authority over the product vision, strategic direction, business model, governance model, intellectual property, and future platform extensions.

## Founder Rights

The Founder retains:

- **Final authority:** final decision-making authority across Prontera Commerce.
- **Product vision authority:** authority over the product vision, user experience direction, marketplace concept, and long-term roadmap.
- **Strategic authority:** authority over platform strategy, market direction, partnerships, expansion priorities, and major technical direction.
- **Business authority:** authority over business model decisions, commercial strategy, pricing direction, monetization strategy, and merchant success strategy.
- **Governance authority:** authority over platform governance, Human-in-the-Loop rules, AI agent boundaries, approval workflows, and decision escalation rules.

## Human-in-the-Loop Model

Prontera Commerce follows a Human-in-the-Loop governance model.

AI agents may assist with analysis, recommendations, documentation, customer support, merchant onboarding, operational workflows, and low-risk automation.

AI agents may recommend but cannot override Founder decisions.

AI agents may not independently approve or execute high-impact decisions that affect financial, legal, compliance, policy, moderation, pricing, strategic, or irreversible operational outcomes.

## AI Agent Boundaries

AI agents such as Mr.P, CEO AI, CFO AI, CMO AI, support agents, and future marketplace agents may support platform operations within the boundaries set by the Founder.

They may:

- Analyze data.
- Generate recommendations.
- Draft documentation.
- Assist users and merchants.
- Automate low-risk routine operations.
- Prepare execution plans for human review.

They may not:

- Override Founder decisions.
- Approve high-impact decisions.
- Execute irreversible actions without approval.
- Change platform policy without human authorization.
- Make binding legal, financial, compliance, or strategic decisions.

## Founder Final Authority

The Founder has final authority over Prontera Commerce and may approve, reject, pause, reverse, or override platform decisions, AI recommendations, operational workflows, and future governance processes.
