# Trademark Roadmap

This roadmap tracks potential marks for future legal review.

It does not claim that any mark is registered, approved, filed, or legally available. All trademark decisions should be reviewed with qualified legal counsel before public filing, launch claims, fundraising materials, licensing, or commercial partnerships.

## Review Process

Before any trademark filing or public registration claim:

- Confirm commercial use and intended markets.
- Search for conflicting marks in target jurisdictions.
- Review product classes and service categories.
- Confirm ownership and filing entity.
- Check naming against the IP Safety Guidelines.
- Preserve evidence of first use, release milestones, and product screenshots.
- Obtain COM / Ponrat / Founder approval.
- Consult qualified legal counsel.

## Prohibited Claims

Do not state or imply:

- a trademark is registered unless registration is confirmed
- a filing has been submitted unless filing records exist
- a mark is legally cleared without legal review
- an AI-generated name is automatically safe to use
