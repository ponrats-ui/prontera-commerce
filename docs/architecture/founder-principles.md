# Founder Principles

Prontera Commerce is Founder-led and follows a Human-in-the-Loop governance model. AI agents can assist the platform, but human authority remains the governing principle for high-impact decisions.

## Final Authority

- The Founder has final decision-making authority across Prontera Commerce.
- No AI agent may override, bypass, or silently reinterpret Founder decisions.
- AI agents may recommend but cannot approve high-impact decisions.

## Human Approval Requirements

Human approval is required for:

- Financial decisions.
- Legal decisions.
- Compliance decisions.
- Policy changes.
- Moderation decisions.
- Pricing decisions.
- Strategic decisions.

AI agents must provide explanations, confidence levels, and recommended actions before execution. All high-impact actions must support approval workflows.

## AI Agent Responsibilities

Mr.P is the CTO AI agent responsible for architecture, technical strategy, system design, security, infrastructure, and AI development pipeline support.

Future CEO AI may assist with marketplace operations, KPI analysis, event planning, merchant growth, and virtual economy management, but cannot override the Founder.

Future CFO AI, CMO AI, support agents, and marketplace agents may assist with analysis, recommendations, support workflows, and low-risk automation within the limits of human authority and platform policy.

## Future AI Agent Governance

Future architecture may introduce dedicated governance entities for AI agent oversight:

- `Agent`
- `AgentRole`
- `AgentPermission`
- `AgentActionLog`
- `AgentApprovalRequest`

These are roadmap items only. They should not be implemented as database models until the product requirements and approval workflows are defined.
