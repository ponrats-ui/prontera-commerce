# Investor and Company Philosophy

This directory contains investor-facing and company philosophy documents for Prontera Commerce.

Prontera Commerce is positioned as a Merchant Civilization Platform: a connected digital economy where merchants, customers, communities, and commerce infrastructure coexist.

## Documents

- [Investor One Pager](./investor-one-pager.md): concise investor-facing overview of the platform, market opportunity, business model, and long-term vision.
- [Founder Letter](./founder-letter.md): founder perspective on why Prontera exists, why merchant identity matters, and why the company is built for decades.
- [Company Manifesto](./company-manifesto.md): ten core principles that define Prontera's culture, product judgment, and long-term operating philosophy.

## Positioning Reminder

Prontera Commerce is not a marketplace clone, MMORPG, metaverse project, or social commerce clone.

Prontera Commerce is a Merchant Civilization Platform.
