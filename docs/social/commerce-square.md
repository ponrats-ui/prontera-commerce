# Commerce Square

## Role

Commerce Square is the homepage of the Prontera civilization.

It answers:

- What happened today?
- Which merchants are active?
- What are my friends doing?
- Which guilds are gathering?
- What event is next?
- Which region should I visit?

## Feed Types

- Merchant announcements
- New store openings
- Founder updates
- Featured products
- World news
- Guild activities
- Live events

## Daily Life

- Daily visitor greeting
- Featured merchant
- Region spotlight
- Daily discovery prompt
- Daily commerce news

Daily life is designed to encourage curiosity and belonging, not compulsive
spending.

## Ranking Principles

Future feed ranking should prioritize:

- Followed merchants
- Friends and guilds
- Regional relevance
- Freshness
- Trust and safety
- User controls

Paid promotion must be labeled and must not impersonate community activity.

## Social AI

The AI Town Guide explains the square and introduces published people, places,
guilds, and events. It does not act as a friend, send messages, or make decisions
for users.
