# Customer Reputation System

## Goal

Helpful customers are visible participants in the commerce civilization.

## Foundation Factors

- Helpful reviews
- Community participation
- Event attendance
- Merchant recommendations

Sprint 17 stores these factors locally and displays a foundation score.

## Titles

- Traveler
- Citizen
- Trusted Customer
- Commerce Ambassador
- Legend of Prontera

## Safety Principles

Customer reputation must not:

- Determine access to essential customer support
- Remove refund or legal rights
- Encourage spending for status
- Penalize legitimate complaints
- Expose sensitive purchase history
- Become a hidden credit score
