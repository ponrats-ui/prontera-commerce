# Founder Museum

The Founder Museum preserves Prontera’s early civic memory.

It extends Founder Hall from a recognition page into a permanent historical
landmark.

## Museum sections

- Founder Timeline
- Project Milestones
- Founder Merchants
- Alpha Contributors
- AI Founder Council
- Historical Screenshot Exhibits

## Historical screenshots

Sprint 18A uses styled exhibit frames as placeholders until curated screenshots
are selected. This avoids adding fake screenshots while still giving the museum
a sense of archive and place.

## Purpose

Founder Museum should make early builders feel remembered.

It is not a leaderboard. It is a civic archive.

The emotional goal is:

```text
I was here when the first roads opened.
```
