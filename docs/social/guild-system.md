# Guild System

## Goal

Guilds provide durable community identity around shared commerce interests,
regions, and skills.

## Guild Types

- Merchant Guild
- Creator Guild
- Technology Guild
- Logistics Guild
- Artisan Guild
- Regional Guild

## Guild Contract

- Guild ID
- Name
- Type
- Hall
- Member count
- Reputation
- Announcement
- Ranking
- Visual identity

Sprint 17 allows local join and leave interactions.

## Initial Guilds

- Central Merchant Guild
- Makers' Hearth
- Spark Collective
- Blue Route League
- Open Studio Circle
- Six Roads Fellowship

## Reputation

Guild reputation is display-only in Sprint 17. It communicates community trust
and activity but does not unlock commercial privileges.

Future reputation should consider:

- Helpful shared resources
- Member participation
- Event quality
- Merchant trust
- Governance record
- Longevity

## Future Expansion

- Guild markets
- Guild events
- Guild commerce festivals
- Member roles and permissions
- Moderation
- Regional chapters
- Shared merchant halls

Guilds must never coordinate price fixing, exclusionary conduct, harassment, or
unsafe commerce behavior.
