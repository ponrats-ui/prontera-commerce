# Prontera Commerce Business Model

## Revenue Streams

1. Subscription
- Starter: 299 THB/month
- Pro: 999 THB/month
- Business: 2,999 THB/month
- Enterprise: Custom

2. Transaction Fee
1-3% per successful order

3. Premium Location
Virtual shop rental in high-traffic zones

4. Advertising
In-world ads, sponsored NPCs, featured shops

5. AI Services
AI Merchant, AI Support, AI Analytics

## Core Business Direction

Prontera Commerce is a Merchant OS + Marketplace + Virtual World + AI Commerce Platform.

The first revenue goal is to acquire paying merchants before building the full MMORPG layer.
