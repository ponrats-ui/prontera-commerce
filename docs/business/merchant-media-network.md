# Merchant Media Network

## Vision

Transform merchant locations into digital commercial real estate.

Every merchant storefront becomes a discoverable location.

Every location can become a visibility asset.

Prontera Commerce is not only a Merchant OS and Merchant Civilization Platform. It is also a merchant-focused advertising and visibility network.

## Problem

Traditional marketplaces monetize through:

- sponsored products
- search advertising
- algorithm boosting

These models primarily promote products. They do not strengthen merchant identity.

In many marketplaces, advertising helps one product win a click. It does not help the merchant build a durable place, recognizable storefront, community presence, or long-term brand memory.

## Solution

Prontera promotes merchants through location-based visibility.

Advertising is integrated into the commerce world.

Merchants do not only buy clicks. They establish presence.

The Merchant Media Network turns world discovery into a revenue and ecosystem layer. Visibility can be attached to storefronts, buildings, districts, warp gates, live status, Founder Merchant showcases, and high-traffic commerce routes.

## Advertising Inventory

### 1. Rooftop Billboard

Premium billboard placement above merchant buildings.

Examples:

- Official Store branding
- product campaigns
- seasonal promotions

### 2. Store Banner Ads

Store Banner Ads are visible on storefronts and support merchant campaigns, promotions, launches, or official brand messaging.

### 3. District Sponsorship

District Sponsorship gives brands or merchants premium visibility inside commerce districts.

Examples:

- Tech Bazaar sponsored by NVIDIA
- Harbor District sponsored by Maersk
- Artisan Valley sponsored by Canon

All sponsorship examples are conceptual and do not imply any existing partnership.

### 4. Warp Gate Advertising

Warp Gate Advertising creates sponsored visibility during fast travel between cities, districts, market zones, and commerce hubs.

This turns movement through the Commerce Gate Network into a measurable visibility moment.

### 5. Featured Merchant Placement

Featured Merchant Placement gives selected merchants premium placement inside discovery pages, world map views, district pages, and category surfaces.

### 6. Founder District Showcase

Founder District Showcase highlights selected Founder Merchants in high-trust, high-visibility areas of the world experience.

## Official Store Program

Official Store is a future merchant status concept for trusted, verified, or premium storefronts.

Benefits may include:

- verification badge
- premium storefront
- rooftop billboard access
- featured placement
- enhanced analytics

Official Store should increase merchant trust and visibility without weakening customer transparency or platform integrity.

## Governance

The Merchant Media Network must remain merchant-first.

Advertising should be transparent, explainable, and clearly distinguished from organic discovery where required. It should increase merchant visibility without becoming opaque manipulation.

Future implementation should include review rules for brand safety, IP safety, placement conflicts, customer trust, and Human-in-the-Loop approval for high-impact visibility decisions.
